Rotation

  • You can make a sprite rotate by setting its rotation property to a value in radians
  • The anchor point you've seen that a sprites top left corner represents its x and y position
  • The rotation will happen around the spirtes anchor point
  • To change the anchor point to the centre, use the 0.5;
  • The anchor.x and anchor.y values represent a percentage of the textures dimensions from 0 to 1 (0% to 100%)
  • Spirtes also have a pivot property which works in a similar way to anchor
  • Pivot sets the position of the sprites x/y origin point
