Size and Scale

  • You can change a sprites by setting its widfth and height properties
  • Sprites also have scale.x and scale.y properties that change the sprites width and height proportionately
  • Scale values are numbers between 0 and 1 that represents a percentage of the sprites size.
    • 1 means 100% (full size)
    • 0.5 means 50% (haf size)
    • 2 doubles the size
